C. Joseph Vijay, popularly known as Vijay, is a renowned Indian actor and playback singer primarily in Tamil cinema. Known as "Thalapathy" by fans, he has delivered numerous blockbusters, earning accolades for his charismatic screen presence, versatile acting, and philanthropy. Vijay is one of Tamil cinema's most influential and celebrated stars.
